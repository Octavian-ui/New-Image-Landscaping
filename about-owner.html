Meet Lenny

Trained designer. Owner-operator.

— The Owner

Lenny Ritts

Founder of New Image Landscaping. Born and raised in Western New York, Lenny has been working outdoor properties across Hamburg, Orchard Park, West Seneca, Boston, and Blasdell since 2015. What started as a one-man operation with a single truck has grown into a trusted name across the Southtowns — but the work still gets done the same way: by hand, on time, and with a straight quote up front.

What sets Lenny apart is his eye. He earned a Bachelor of Fine Arts with a concentration in Interior Design from Buffalo State University in 2011, and he's been applying that design training to outdoor spaces ever since. Where most landscapers see a yard, Lenny sees composition — how plantings frame a walkway, how a single accent boulder anchors a bed, how color and texture work together across the seasons. Every install is sketched out with intention before the first shovel hits the ground.

When you hire New Image, you're not getting passed off to a subcontractor or a rotating crew. You're getting Lenny — on the estimate, on the design, on the install, and on every call after.
